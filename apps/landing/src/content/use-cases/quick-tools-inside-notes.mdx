## Where this workflow fits

Philo is designed for people solving temporary problems while planning. The product direction is about keeping the loop between writing, deciding, and running as short as possible.

## Capability checklist

- widget library: library/ under the vault root or resolved journal root
- Daily notes do not inline the full widget payload.
- Notes, images, Excalidraw files, widget files, widget-library files, and chat-history files live under the resolved content directory.
- Philo makes widgets cheap to create, easy to delete, and reusable only when they earn it.
- changing vaultDir does not migrate existing widget files, library items, or chat-history files into the new root

## Why this workflow feels lighter

Daily notes do not inline the full widget payload.

Notes, images, Excalidraw files, widget files, widget-library files, and chat-history files live under the resolved content directory.
