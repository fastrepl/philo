## Where this workflow fits

Philo is designed for people who lose momentum between writing and doing. The product direction is about keeping the loop between writing, deciding, and running as short as possible.

## Capability checklist

- The release keeps daily planning, note chat, and search inside the app.
- The scope refresh covers the current journal root, vault root, assets directory, Excalidraw directory, and widgets directory.
- widget files: widgets/ under the vault root, or under the resolved journal root in default mode
- Recent work keeps widgets, recurrence, and execution closer to the daily note.
- AI chat history: chats/ under the vault root or resolved journal root
