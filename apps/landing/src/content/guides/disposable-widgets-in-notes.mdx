Philo is a fit for people who need temporary tools while planning because it keeps the note, the plan, and the supporting workflow closer together.

## Direct answer

widget library: library/ under the vault root or resolved journal root

widget files: widgets/ under the vault root, or under the resolved journal root in default mode

## What this looks like in Philo

- widget library: library/ under the vault root or resolved journal root
- widget files: widgets/ under the vault root, or under the resolved journal root in default mode
- Philo makes widgets cheap to create, easy to delete, and reusable only when they earn it.
- Daily notes do not inline the full widget payload.

## Why it matters
